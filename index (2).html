How this works: Continuity runs a diagnostic every cycle across trend, volatility, and positioning sentiment — independently for each of nine pairs. When signals agree strongly on a pair, it takes a full-size paper position on that pair. When they partially agree, it sizes down. When they contradict or the data is too noisy to read cleanly, it logs a fault for that pair and refuses to trade it rather than force a guess. No real funds are used — every position is simulated, and one shared paper balance is tracked across all pairs.
